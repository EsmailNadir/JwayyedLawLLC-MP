## Project Overview

**Jwayyed Law LLC** – marketing website for an Ohio law firm based in Columbus.

- Built with **Next.js App Router** and **TypeScript**
- **Static content-as-code**: all marketing, service, and blog content lives directly in page files (`app/*`) or data files (`data/*`) – **no CMS** or database
- Focused on:
  - Criminal defense & OVI/DUI
  - Personal injury
  - Civil litigation & landlord–tenant
  - Business formation & disputes
  - Estate planning & trusts

The site must balance:

- **Clean, maintainable code**
- **Strong SEO & indexing**
- **Clear, trustworthy content** for legal consumers in Ohio
